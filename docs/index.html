The Big Idea

Dancing Pythons is a fun tool for dancing with friends at a party! Our goal was to create a live visual that places a silhouette of you dancing over moving art that is generated based on the mood of the song!

How it works

There are three components to this project: analyzing a song to generate an accurate mood, developing computational moving art based on that mood, and detecting edges and overlaying them onto the moving art background. The first component uses Spotify's API to analyze how happy, sad, energetic, etc. the song is. The second part generates art using only colors that fit the given mood. The third component uses Open CV's Canny Edge Detection algorithm to generate black and white frames as seen here. Image addition is then used to bring all of these components together.

How we got here

We began by creating a minimal viable project. For us, that was getting Open CV to detect edges and produce a trace. Then, we attempted to place the silhouette over art and generate art based on mood. Once we could do this using single images, we developed moving art that corresponded to the tempo of the music playing. Lastly, we worked on placing the sihouette over moving art.
